About Us

Hebron Prayer House Ministries was established in 1984 through the divine calling and unwavering faith of our beloved founder, Sis. C. Nesam. With a heart devoted to God and a passion for reaching souls, she began this ministry with a simple yet powerful vision—to lead people into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ through prayer, worship, and the teaching of God's Word. What started as a humble gathering of believers has, by God's grace, grown into a vibrant ministry that continues to impact countless lives.


The ministry has always been founded on the promise of Matthew 16:18, where Jesus declared, "I will build My Church, and the gates of Hades shall not prevail against it." This promise has been the guiding foundation of Hebron Prayer House Ministries, strengthening the church through every season and reminding us that God Himself is the builder and sustainer of His people.


In loving memory of Sis. C. Nesam, the church building of Hebron Prayer House was established as a lasting testimony to her vision, faith, and lifelong dedication to the Kingdom of God. The foundation stone was laid by Deacon M. Jayaraj on 10th April 2019, marking the beginning of a new chapter in the ministry's journey. The completed church building was officially opened by Amma S. Yagammal, Founder and President of Berachah Prayer House, and prayerfully dedicated to the glory of God by Pastor A. Thomas Raj of A.C.A. Avadi Ministries.


Today, under the faithful leadership of Pastor J. Immanuel Devaraj, President, and Sis. I. Violet Rani, Secretary, Hebron Prayer House Ministries continues to uphold its biblical foundation while embracing the opportunities to serve a changing world. Our church is committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers in spiritual maturity, supporting families through prayer and fellowship, and extending God's love through compassionate outreach.


Located in Royapuram, Chennai, Hebron Prayer House is more than a place of worship—it is a spiritual family where people from every background are welcomed with love, encouraged in faith, and equipped to live according to God's Word. As we reflect on God's faithfulness over the years, we remain committed to carrying forward the vision entrusted to us, believing that the best is yet to come. With grateful hearts, we continue to build lives, strengthen families, and glorify Jesus Christ, who is the true foundation and everlasting Head of His Church.
