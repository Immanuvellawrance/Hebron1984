About Us

Hebron Prayer House is a Christ-centered church dedicated to worship, prayer, discipleship, and sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Located in Royapuram, Chennai, our mission is to lead people into a deeper relationship with God through Biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and a life transformed by the Holy Spirit.


Under the leadership of Pastor J. Immanuvel Devaraj, Hebron Prayer House serves individuals and families through worship services, prayer meetings, Bible studies, and outreach ministries.
